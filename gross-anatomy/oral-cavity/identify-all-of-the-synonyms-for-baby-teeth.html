Identify ALL of the synonyms for “baby teeth".

(A) ** primary teeth

(B) juvenile teeth

(C) ** deciduous teeth

(D) ** milk teeth

(E) ** temporary teeth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deciduous teeth-also known as baby teeth, primary teeth, or milk teeth-are your first teeth. They start developing during the embryonic stage and start to erupt through the gums about 6 months after birth. All 20 of them are typically in by age 2½.

Concept note-2: -Baby teeth are also known as milk teeth, temporary teeth or deciduous teeth.

Concept note-3: -Primary teeth are the official dental term for baby teeth. Primary teeth can also be called milk teeth and deciduous teeth. These are the first set of teeth that come in for a child and are the precursors the permanent adult teeth that come in during a child’s teen years.

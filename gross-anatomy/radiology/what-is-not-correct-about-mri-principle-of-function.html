What is not correct about MRI principle of function

(A) a high intensity magnetic field is created around body to align the protons

(B) external radio-frequency of the same frequency as the spinning protons is applied

(C) ** a gauntry revolves around the body at a high spinning rate

(D) released energy is then received as a radio wave and converted by computer to create image

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The strong magnetic field created by the MRI scanner causes the atoms in your body to align in the same direction. Radio waves are then sent from the MRI machine and move these atoms out of the original position. As the radio waves are turned off, the atoms return to their original position and send back radio signals.

Concept note-2: -An MRI system consists of four major components: a main magnet formed by superconducting coils, gradient coils, radiofrequency (RF) coils, and computer systems. Each component has safety considerations.

Concept note-3: -2) A major advantage of MRI is: the ease with which equipment is updated or replaced. its relatively low cost, compared to CT scans.

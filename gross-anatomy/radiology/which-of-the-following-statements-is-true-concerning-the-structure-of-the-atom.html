which of the following statements is true concerning the structure of the atom>

(A) the nucleus protons and electrons

(B) electrons have no weight or mass

(C) ** the orbtital path of an electron around the nucleus is called an electron shell

(D) electrons are unstable in their orbits and shift freely

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among the given statements, the true statement about the structure of an atom is C. the nucleus contains protons and neutrons. Explanation: In an atom, the number of protons is not always equal to the number of neutrons in the nucleus of the atom.

Concept note-2: -The answer is (a) An atom has an equal number of electrons and protons. Explanation: An atom is electrically neutral because the number of protons is always equal to a number of electrons. Hence option a) is right.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following combination of statements is true regarding the interpretation of the atomic orbitals? a) An electron in an orbital of high angular momentum stays away from the nucleus than an electron in the orbital of lower angular momentum.

Concept note-4: -Finally, which statement best describes an atomic orbital? The answer is (B), a three-dimensional mathematical description of the most likely location of an electron in an atom.

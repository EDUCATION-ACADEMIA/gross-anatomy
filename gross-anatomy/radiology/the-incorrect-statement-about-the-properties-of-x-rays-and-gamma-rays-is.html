The incorrect statement about the properties of X Rays and Gamma Rays, is:

(A) ** Has penetrating power because it has a very long wavelength

(B) Experiencing attenuation (weakening) when touching the material

(C) Has a scatter (scatter rays)

(D) Has a fluorescent effect

(E) Has a phosphorescence effect

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -X-rays and gamma rays have the same basic properties but come from different parts of the atom. X-rays are emitted from processes outside the nucleus, but gamma rays originate inside the nucleus. They also are generally lower in energy and, therefore less penetrating than gamma rays.

Concept note-2: -X-rays have longer wavelengths than gamma rays but smaller wavelengths and therefore higher energy than ultraviolet radiation. They have been used in various applications in science and industry and are primarily used in medicine for instance in radiography.

Concept note-3: -The great penetrating power of gamma rays stems from the fact that they have no electric charge and thus do not interact with matter as strongly as do charged particles.

Concept note-4: -Properties of X-Rays and Gamma Rays They are not detected by human senses (cannot be seen, heard, felt, etc.). They travel in straight lines at the speed of light. Their paths cannot be changed by electrical or magnetic fields. They can be diffracted to a small degree at interfaces between two different materials.

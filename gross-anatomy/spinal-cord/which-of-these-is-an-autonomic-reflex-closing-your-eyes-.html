Which of these is an autonomic reflex? Closing your eyes ____

(A) during a scary movie

(B) when you’re thinking hard

(C) ** when you sneeze

(D) when you’re trying to fall asleep

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sneezing includes an autonomic reflex that closes your eyes. An autonomic reflex is a motor action that your body makes in response to a stimulus. It doesn’t involve a conscious decision on your part to take that action.

Concept note-2: -Yet, we still close our eyes, why? It has to do with our nervous system. When your brain send the message to your nose that “We need to clear this place out” and you sneeze, this action causes stimulation of the nerves in your head and along the way your eyes are involuntarily told to blink.

Concept note-3: -Sneezing is a vital respiratory reflex frequently associated with allergic rhinitis and viral respiratory infections.

Concept note-4: -Sneezing is a protective reflex, and is sometimes a sign of various medical conditions. Sneezing has been a remarkable sign throughout the history.

Concept note-5: -“This is an involuntary reflex, ” explains Moritz. “When our brain sends this muscle message, one part of the message is to close our eyes. It’s similar to a deep tendon reflex.”

(A) Physiology

(B) Cytology

(C) Embryology

(D) ** Anatomy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Anatomy refers to the internal and external structures of the body and their physical relationships, whereas physiology refers to the study of the functions of those structures.

Concept note-2: -Anatomy is the science that studies the structure of the body. On this page, you’ll find links to descriptions and pictures of the human body’s parts and organ systems from head to toe.

Concept note-3: -Anatomy is the study of the structure and relationship between body parts. Physiology is the study of the function of body parts and the body as a whole.

Concept note-4: -Human anatomy, also known as anthropotomy, which is the anatomical study of the human body.

Concept note-5: -What is Anatomy and Physiology? Anatomy is the study of the structures associated with the human body. Physiology is the study of the function of each of these structures.

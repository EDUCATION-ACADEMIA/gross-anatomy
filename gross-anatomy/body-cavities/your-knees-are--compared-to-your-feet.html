Your knees are ____ compared to your feet.

(A) ** Proximal

(B) Distal

(C) Superficial

(D) Deep

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The knee is proximal to the foot. Moving distally from the hip brings you to the thigh. The foot is distal to the knee. The skin is superficial to bone.

Concept note-2: -Or, similarly, the femur is proximal to the knee, which is proximal to the ankle, which is proximal to the toes.

Concept note-3: -The foot is distal to the knee. The foot can be described as inferior to the knee but the term that best fits the comparison refers to the relation of the foot to the knee as part of the lower limb. The proximal end of the lower limb is the hip and the knee is distal to the hip but proximal to the foot.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: A. The knee is proximal to the ankle.

Concept note-5: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is option c because on the limbs, distal means further from the abdomen whereas proximal means closer to the abdomen. Since the ankle is further than the knee from the abdomen, it is distal to the knee.

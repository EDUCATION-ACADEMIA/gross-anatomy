What type of nerve is most common?

(A) Sensory

(B) ** Motor

(C) Both

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Motor neurons have the most common type of ‘body plan’ for a nerve cell-they are multipolar, each with one axon and several dendrites.

Concept note-2: -Motor neurone disease (MND) is a rare condition that progressively damages parts of the nervous system. This leads to muscle weakness, often with visible wasting. Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) is the most common form of MND.

Concept note-3: -Multipolar neurons are the most common type of neuron. They are located in the central nervous system (brain and spinal cord) and in autonomic ganglia. Multipolar neurons have more than two processes emanating from the neuron cell body.

Concept note-4: -The motor nerve supplying the muscle is actually a bundle of axons from motor neurons carrying excitation to the muscle and sensory nerve fibers that return information to the central nervous system about the state of the muscle.

Concept note-5: -A motor nerve is a nerve that transmits motor signals from the central nervous system (CNS) to the muscles of the body. This is different from the motor neuron, which includes a cell body and branching of dendrites, while the nerve is made up of a bundle of axons.

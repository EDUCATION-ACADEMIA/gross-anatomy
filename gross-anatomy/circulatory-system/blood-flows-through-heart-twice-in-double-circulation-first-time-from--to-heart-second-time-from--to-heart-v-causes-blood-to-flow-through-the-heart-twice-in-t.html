CIRCULATORY SYSTEM

Blood flows through heart twice in double circulation. First time from ____ to heart, second time from ____ to heart. V causes blood to flow through the heart twice in the circulation, the first time from ____ and the second time from ____.

(A) Lungs, body organs

(B) Skin, Lungs

(C) ** Body organs, lungs

(D) Lungs, skin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is called a double circulatory system because blood passes through the heart twice per circuit. The right pump sends deoxygenated blood to the lungs where it becomes oxygenated and returns back to the heart. The left pump sends the newly oxygenated blood around the body.

Concept note-2: -The term used for blood going through the heart twice during each cycle is double circulation.

Concept note-3: -The heart works as a double circulatory system. This means two pumps that work at the same time to pump blood in two different directions. The right-hand side of the heart collects deoxygenated blood from the body and pumps it to the lungs (to collect more oxygen).

Concept note-4: -It beats around 100, 000 times daily.

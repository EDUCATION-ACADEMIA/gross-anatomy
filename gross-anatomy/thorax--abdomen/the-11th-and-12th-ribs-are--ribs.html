THORAX ABDOMEN

[SOURCES]
The 11th and 12th ribs are ____ ribs.

(A) ** floating

(B) true

(C) complete

(D) fused

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The sternum is not attached to the last two pairs of ribs at the bottom of the rib cage. These ribs are known as “floating ribs ” because their only attachment is at the rear of the rib cage, where they are connected to the spine’s vertebrae. Floating ribs are the 11th and 12th pairs of ribs.

Concept note-2: -11th and 12th pairs of ribs are called floating ribs as they are not connected to sternum on the ventral side. They are attached to vertebral column on the dorsal aspect but left free on the ventral aspect. So they are called Floating ribs.

Concept note-3: -The floating ribs (ribs 11 and 12) are not connected to the sternum. In addition to these anatomical differences, the twelfth rib is shorter than the other ribs and has only a single articular facet.

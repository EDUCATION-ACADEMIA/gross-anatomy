Which of the following statements regarding the conduction system of the heart is NOT true?

(A) ** The AV node stimulates the SA node.

(B) The conduction system stimulates the different chambers of the heart in the correct order.

(C) Purkinje fibers stimulate the ventricular myocardium.

(D) The heart rate is regulated by the autonomic nervous system.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atrioventricular valve is not part of the intrinsic conduction system. The AV valve functions to prevent backflow of blood in the heart. Cardiac impulses are originated with the SA node, carried through AV node and bundle branches to the ventricles.

Concept note-2: -The coronary sinus is not part of the heartâ€™s conduction system.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following statements is true about the SA (sinoatrial) node? The pacemaker cells, which establish the heart rate, are located in the SA node.

Concept note-4: -Your heart (cardiac) conduction system sends the signal to start a heartbeat. It also sends signals that tell different parts of your heart to relax and contract (squeeze). This process of contracting and relaxing controls blood flow through your heart and to the rest of your body.

What part of the Intrinsic Conduction System is located inside the interventricular septum?

(A) Purkinje Fibers

(B) SA & AV Nodes

(C) ** Bundle Branches

(D) Superior Vena Cava

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: The portion of the intrinsic conduction system located in the interventricular septum is the AV bundle.

Concept note-2: -Purkinje fibers They send electrical signals very quickly to your right and left heart ventricles. Your Purkinje fibers are in the subendocardial surface of your ventricle walls.

Concept note-3: -The main parts of the system are the SA node, AV node, bundle of HIS, bundle branches, and Purkinje fibers.

Concept note-4: -The AV node conducts the impulse of the bundle of His, or interventricular bundle, located in the interventricular septum.

Concept note-5: -Answer and Explanation: The typical conducting tissue of the heart found in the inter-ventricular septum is referred to Bundle of His. The Bundle of His is a significant and necessary component of the electrical heart conduction system.

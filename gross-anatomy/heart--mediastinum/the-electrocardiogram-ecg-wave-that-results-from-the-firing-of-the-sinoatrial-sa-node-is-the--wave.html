HEART MEDIASTINUM

[SOURCES]
The electrocardiogram (ECG) wave that results from the firing of the sinoatrial (SA) node is the ____ wave.

(A) QRS

(B) T

(C) ** P

(D) S-T

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The P wave represents the electrical depolarization of the atria. In a healthy person, this originates at the sinoatrial node (SA node) and disperses into both left and right atria.

Concept note-2: -The P wave indicates atrial depolarization. The P wave occurs when the sinus node, also known as the sinoatrial node, creates an action potential that depolarizes the atria.

Concept note-3: -Einthoven, who had already used A to label the first ventricular event, used P to designate the record made by the electrical activity produced by the atria.

Concept note-4: -Acting as the heart’s natural pacemaker, the SA node “fires” at regular intervals to cause the heart of beat with a rhythmn of about 60 to 70 beats per minute for a healthy, resting heart.

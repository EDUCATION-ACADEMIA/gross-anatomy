Concept note-1: -A bio is a summary description of yourself, your professional and academic background, your accomplishments, and what you aim to achieve. It’s a personalized description of your professional profile and a great way to let others know what value you offer.

Concept note-2: -adjective. bio·positive. ¦bīō + : regenerative. specifically : relating to the phase of radiation damage to living tissue in which repair of previously disrupted tissue takes place (as in the replacing of X-ray-destroyed tumor cells by connective tissue)

Concept note-3: -What is an Instagram bio? An Instagram bio is a small summary, found underneath your username, about yourself or your business. In your Instagram bio, you can include a short description, contact information, emojis, and more, provided you keep it under 150 characters.

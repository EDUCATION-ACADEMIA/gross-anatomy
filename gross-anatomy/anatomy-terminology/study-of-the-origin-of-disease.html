Study of the origin of disease

(A) morphology

(B) cytology

(C) ** etiology

(D) histology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pathogenesis-or “origin of disease”-is the study of the processes that give rise to physiologic dysfunction and illness. In the case of COVID-19, it is the study of virus-induced mischief-how SARS-CoV-2 interacts with various cells, tissues, and organs to cause COVID-19.

Concept note-2: -Etiology is also the name for the study of the causes of diseases. It can also refer to the study of the cause of things in other fields, such as philosophy and physics. But it is most commonly used in the context of medicine. In British English, it is spelled aetiology.

Concept note-3: -Etiology describes the cause or causes of a disease.

Concept note-4: -Etiology is defined as the scientific study of the cause of diseases.

Concept note-5: -Etiology is the study of the cause of disease while Epidemiology deals with the cause as well as the spread of disease.

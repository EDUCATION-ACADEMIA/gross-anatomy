PEARL stands for

(A) Pupils Equal Asymmetrical Reactive Light

(B) ** Pupils Equal And Reactive to Light

(C) Pupil Equal Astigmatism Retina Lens

(D) Pupils Equal A Real Lens

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can also think of PERRLA as a sentence. Pupils are equal, round, and reactive to light and accommodation.

Concept note-2: -To document the findings of a pupillary examination, many students and doctors use PERRLA, which stands for: Pupils Equal, Round, Reactive to Light and Accommodation.

Concept note-3: -The pupils are generally equal in size. They constrict to direct illumination (direct response) and to illumination of the opposite eye (consensual response). The pupil dilates in the dark. Both pupils constrict when the eye is focused on a near object (accommodative response).
